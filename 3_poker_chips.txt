We are in charge of chip production for a casino. Management would like to make these new chips using the on-hand inventory of raw material. The denominations we can make are (in dollars) 1, 5, 10, 25, 100, 500, 1000. Each denomination of chip requires a different amount of several raw materials. We are limited to the amount of material on hand, consisting of clay, lead, silver and gold. 

Objective: Manufacture the **highest possible total value** in poker chips. 

Constraints: 
- Each chip's requires a specific amount of materials, found in recipes.csv, 
- the total amount is in the file ing_amounts.csv
